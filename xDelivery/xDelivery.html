I participated in a web design contest on DesignCrowd where I proposed a redesign for xDelivery.com, a logistics and delivery service platform. The aim was to give the site a more modern and user-friendly interface that reflects the reliability and efficiency of their services. My concept focused on improving the visual hierarchy, enhancing the user experience, and creating a responsive layout that works seamlessly across all devices. Although my design was not selected, the project allowed me to explore solutions for a real-world business, refine my design process under a brief, and deliver a polished and thoughtful concept. This submission remains a valuable addition to my portfolio, showcasing my ability to design for service-oriented platforms.
