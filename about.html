About

Get to Know Me, My Approach,

Approach

My Approach for Design and Development

01.

I start by understanding the problem and the target users. I gather information through user interviews, competitor analysis, and stakeholder discussions. This helps me define user needs, pain points, and goals, ensuring my design decisions are backed by real insights.

02.

I organize content and define how users will navigate through the product. I create user flows, site maps, and task flows to ensure the structure makes sense and supports a smooth, logical experience for the user.

03.

I sketch low-fidelity wireframes to focus on layout and functionality without getting distracted by visuals. Then, I build interactive prototypes to simulate user interactions, test user journeys, and communicate ideas clearly with developers or stakeholders.

04.

I design high-fidelity screens using typography, color, spacing, and imagery that align with the brand identity. I focus on creating a clean, consistent, and visually appealing interface while making sure it's accessible and user-friendly.

05.

I test my designs with real users or team members, gather feedback, and analyze usability issues. Based on insights, I refine and iterate on the design to improve the user experience before final handoff to development.

06.

I bring the final designs to life. I pay close attention to responsiveness, accessibility, and performance. I make sure the implemented design matches the visuals and functions smoothly across devices. I also collaborate with other developers if needed to ensure a seamless launch.
