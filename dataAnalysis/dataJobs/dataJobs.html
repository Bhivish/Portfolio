In this project, I analyzed a comprehensive dataset on data-related jobs, including roles like Data Analyst, Data Scientist, and Data Engineer. Using Power BI and Power Query, I explored key trends in salaries, job popularity, company size, and regional demand across various states. The dashboard features interactive visualizations such as bar charts, maps, and tables, providing insights into which jobs pay the most, where demand is highest, and how company size impacts salaries.

Some High Fidelity Mockups

To enhance the visual appeal of the dashboard, I designed a custom background template in Figma, incorporating a modern and professional aesthetic. I exported the design as an image and seamlessly integrated it into Power BI as a dashboard background. This personalized touch elevated the overall user experience, making the dashboard not only insightful but also visually engaging.
